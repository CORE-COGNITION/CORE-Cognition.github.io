% --- About the group -------------------------------------------------------
\section*{About the group}
We work towards a \textbf{unified theory of human cognition}, taking the form of
\textbf{computational models} that can simulate, predict, and explain human behavior
across a wide range of situations. The group is part of the
\href{https://www.kyb.tuebingen.mpg.de/en}{Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics}
in Tübingen, a vibrant student town at
the foot of the Swabian alps and one of Europe's leading hubs for cognitive 
science and machine learning research. More information
on our \href{https://XXX}{lab webpage}.

% --- About the position ----------------------------------------------------
\section*{About the position}
You will contribute to research in one of the lab's directions:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Foundation models of human cognition
  \item Modeling human behavior in naturalistic environments (e.g. conversations, games, coding)
  \item Automated scientific discovery for the cognitive sciences
  \item AI alignment
 
\end{itemize}

% --- Requirements ----------------------------------------------------------
\section*{What we are looking for}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Enrolled in a Master's program (or an advanced Bachelor's program) in cognitive science, computer science, machine learning, psychology, or a related field.
  \item Intellectual curiosity, initiative, and the ability to communicate clearly.
  \item Programming experience, ideally in Python.
  \item Prior research experience in machine learning, cognitive science, or a related area is a plus.
  \item Willingness to work on-site in Tübingen.
\end{itemize}

% --- What we offer ---------------------------------------------------------
\section*{What we offer}
\begin{itemize}
  \item The opportunity to work on frontier research at the intersection of machine learning and cognitive science.
  \item An international research environment with collaborators at DeepMind, Princeton, Harvard, and beyond.
  \item Proven track record of high-impact publications in venues such as Nature, PNAS, NeurIPS, ICLR, and ICML.
  \item Large-scale compute through the Max Planck ecosystem.
  \item Room to shape a young, ambitious, and growing research group. 
\end{itemize}
